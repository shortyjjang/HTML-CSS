Edward Lentsch Culture

Edward Lentsch’s work is in over 500 public and private collections worldwide. His clients include Holly Hunt, Meridith Baer Home, and Nordstrom to name a few. His work is in many celebrity collections including William Shatner and Kelly Rippa along with top designers and style gurus like Lorre Rodkin and Faye Resnick. Lentsch is much more than a painter---he is an author, inventor, designer-builder and founder of Artist League a think-tank for emerging artists. The “Artist’s Giving Pledge” is his personal philanthropic initiative to donate one million dollars in paintings to help both non-profit and for-benefit institutions for fundraising.

Edward Lentsch Story

Edward Lentsch’s expansive artistic practice explores his relationship between the self, nature and the universe. From the Fibonacci sequence to the teachings of Aristotle, Lentsch attempts to create a bridge between the natural world and these intellectual canons, exploring the interconnection between the realms of science and mysticism, the metaphysical and the spiritual. He explores how these three facets are integrated within a global environment and moreover how we, as humans, fit within this complex matrix of thought using the ideas of some of the world’s greatest writers and scholars.
