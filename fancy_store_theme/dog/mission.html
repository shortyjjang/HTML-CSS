All proceeds support our veterans’ programs.

From the early hours of each morning, our bakery is mixing up batches of handmade baked goods while providing valuable work experience for entrepreneurial vets and military spouses.

The Dog Tag work-study program combines education with the experience necessary for our vets to succeed post duty as civilians.

Dog Tag Bakery treats, and its incredible program, are made possible through the loving direction and inspiration of Connie Milstein (Connie’s Baking Co.) and Father Curry (Secrets of Jesuit Bread Making). Please join our cause and learn more at: www.dogtaginc.org.
