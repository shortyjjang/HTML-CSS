Seller Tips

Create collections

Collections allow you to group products by category, theme, or any other way you can think of, and make it easier for customers to find the products  in your store that they'll love. To get started, go to the Collections page under the Products drop-down, or simply click here!
 

Organize your store

You can rearrange the order of products in your store by visiting the Organize Products page under the Products drop-down or by clicking here.

Use lifestyle images

We encourage you to use lifestyle "editorial" images on Fancy. When adding products, try using a lifestyle image as the main image, at the very least, and add accompanying product shots as needed.

Add tags

Add keyword tags to your products to enhance discoverability in search. You can find the Tags section on the right-hand sidebar on the Product Edit page.

Update your sold out inventory

Users can opt to be notified by email and app notification when you restock an item.
