Rare Boutique, located in Southfield, Michigan, is an urban boutique bringing you the freshest kicks and hottest fashion trends. Rare boutique provides the shopper with a unique, one-on-one custom experience. Catering people of all ages and tastes - shoes, clothing, accesories, and jewelry.

Our vision started at a young age when we began collecting sneakers and officially becoming a "sneakerhead". Release days became like Christmas morning and we were hooked. We wanted to share our passion of shoes and our love for Detroit and thus the dream of Rare was born.

Rare not only offers "rare" sneakers, but also offers hand-crafted clothing, fashion-forward urban accessories, and high end jewelry needs.
