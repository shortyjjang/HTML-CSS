No visible branding. No artificial markup. #mypropersneaker

We are an independent design studio making high quality, minimalist product at honest prices, without any artificial markup. We believe in the finest materials and craftmanship. We source our leathers in italy, in the same factories as luxury brands. We sell our products for 1/4 the price of what luxury brands would sell them for.

Applying the principles of alternative minimalism, the brand reduces each design to only the most essential elements in order to respond to the industry’s main question: How to belong to a group and be unique at the same time?

Driving the aesthetics, attitude and spirit of the brand, The Proper Sneaker™ has acquired a reputation for its perfectionism, its simplicity and its unique approach to contemporary fashion shoe establishing a loyal clientele.
