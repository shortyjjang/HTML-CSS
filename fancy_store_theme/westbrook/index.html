Product Attributes

Westbrook Frames adheres to the highest materials and manufacturing standards available.

LENSES

Every frame comes equipped with CR-39 lenses.

  • UV400 Protection
  • Ultra Lightweight
  • Scratch Resistant

HINGES

Each frame is hand assembled with either a double or triple barrel hinge with screws.

ACETATE FRAMES

All Westbrook acetate frames are made from 100% high grade Cellulose Acetate, a bio-plastic sourced from cotton. It is biocompatible, anti-allergenic, biodegradable and renewable. Formed at ambient temperature and pressure, Cellulose Acetate is a molecularly stable material, rendering it the premier choice for prestige quality eyewear.

METAL FRAMES

Every Westbrook metal frame is made of high quality, high density, and yet ultra light, metal alloy.
