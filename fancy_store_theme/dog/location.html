Dog Tag Bakery in Georgetown provides a vehicle for our veterans to put their newly acquired skills into context.
The storefront includes a state of the art bakery and kitchen as well as classroom space. In addition to serving the Georgetown community, Dog Tag Bakery also completes catering and mail orders, allowing Dog Tag fellows to manage a nationwide business.

By facilitating a business rotation through the storefront, fellows will have a chance to learn every aspect of a small business including business and staff management, product creation, customer service, marketing, and operations.

Location

Dog Tag Bakery is located in the heart of Georgetown at 3206 Grace Street NW. (Google Map)
