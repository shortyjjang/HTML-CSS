The Dino Pet photosynthesizes during the day and glows brilliantly when you play with it at night. The alluring blue glow emitted by the Dino Pet is bioluminescence, made from microscopic sea life called dinoflagellates. Far more than a toy, the Dino Pet is a living art piece, introductory pet, biological magic trick, conversation starter and science lesson.
