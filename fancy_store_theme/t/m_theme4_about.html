ABOUT

Cake is a private label brand pushing the boundaries of apparel and objects.

Each item that you see is treated with the utmost care and discipline when it is created for you. Every product represented holds a purity and mystery into even the smallest of details. We invite and encourage you to discover what we make and to be a part of the new.

Cake also facilitates face-to-face collaborations with the most creative people of our time. Notable designers, musicians and artists alike flock to Cake to derive something new and challenge our present.

At Cake we are afraid of nothing, and we will not stop until everything that you wear, see and experience is beautiful.
